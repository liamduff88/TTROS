# /fit-call-prep
> Revisit: when the Fit Call script or offer ladder changes. · Last touched: 2026-07-07

## Purpose
Walk into a free System Fit Call already knowing the prospect's likely revenue leaks. Draft-only; nothing is sent.

## Inputs
- Prospect name, company, website/socials (public signals only).
- Any prior notes in the Business Brain (search prospect entity page first).

## Steps
1. **Research** — public signals only: site, Google profile, reviews, socials, directories. Note business type, team size guess, inbound paths (forms, phone, DMs, booking links), response-speed clues (posted hours, missed-review complaints).
2. **Leak hypotheses** — map 2–3 likely leaks to the three ROI levers: effectiveness (more leads responded/booked), efficiency (less manual admin/chasing), quality (faster response, cleaner handoff). Mark each as hypothesis, not fact.
3. **Question script** — pull the one-call structure from business context §12: revenue path + tools → where leads/calls slow down → what repeated work the team is tired of → what breaks → "if you could fix one workflow first…".
4. **Next-step options** — pre-stage the likely recommendation: Quick-Win Scan (CA$350), Business Efficiency Assessment (CA$750), or a scoped build (Speed-to-Lead / Voice Agent / Client Memory). Include the money-moment question: hand off / work through together / take and implement yourself.
5. **Output** — one-page brief artifact to the queue item; receipt with token count. Update prospect entity page with research only (facts, sourced).

## Never
- Invent prospect facts, urgency, budget, tech stack, or buying intent.
- Contact the prospect or connect any tool to their systems.
- Promise ROI numbers.
- Diagnose everything — the call finds ONE bottleneck.

## Done when
Brief artifact exists with: research summary (sourced), 2–3 leak hypotheses tagged to ROI levers, question script, next-step recommendation options. Receipt written.
