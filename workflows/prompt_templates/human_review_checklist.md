# Human Review Checklist

Use this checklist before any workflow output becomes external, client-facing, published, sent, uploaded, or entered into a CRM.

Prepared run folder:

```text
<prepared_run_folder>
```

Review local files:

- `run_packet.md`
- `intake_template.md` or completed intake file
- `output_placeholder.md` or final output file
- `receipt_placeholder.md` or final receipt file
- source workflow folder referenced in `run_packet.md`

## Checks

- Facts: names, dates, numbers, company details, source references, and workflow assumptions are accurate.
- Claims: outcomes, capabilities, guarantees, compliance statements, and competitive claims are supportable.
- Pricing: prices, discounts, terms, payment timing, and scope boundaries are correct or clearly marked for confirmation.
- Confidential data: private client data, credentials, internal notes, personal data, and sensitive business details are removed or approved for use.
- Client promises: timelines, deliverables, obligations, approvals, and dependencies are realistic and authorized.
- CTA: call to action is clear, appropriate, and not overcommitted.
- Next action: owner, timing, channel, and approval status are clear.
- Formatting: output is readable, complete, and aligned with the source workflow.
- Receipt: closeout notes show what changed, what was validated, and what still needs attention.
- No external action: no send, publish, CRM update, upload, connector action, or client delivery occurs without explicit human approval.
- External action gate: no send, publish, CRM update, upload, connector action, or client delivery occurs without explicit human approval.

Human review result:

```text
APPROVED / NEEDS REVISION / BLOCKED
Reviewer:
Date:
Required changes:
Approved external action, if any:
```
